About the Exhibit

This digital companion extends the physical "Roger Williams Exhibit" at the Peabody Library.

Our Purpose

The physical exhibit displays historical information that is not easily available elsewhere, much of which is too dense or lengthy to fit on gallery walls. This website serves as a permanent extension, providing deeper narratives, student voices, timelines, and primary sources.
