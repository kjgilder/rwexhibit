The 1899 Class Photos

These photographs from 1899 capture three distinct tracks of study at Roger Williams University, representing the graduating year before the Paris Exposition.

Normal Class (1899)

Students training to become teachers — the backbone of Black education across the South.

Academic Class (1899)

The collegiate track offering rigorous academic coursework to prepare graduates for leadership roles.

Ministers Class (1899)

Theological training for Black ministers to serve growing communities across the South.
